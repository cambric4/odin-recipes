French Toast Recipe

Description

French Toast is an easy, quick breakfast meal to make. You truly only need seven ingredients. It only takes about 15 minutes to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp of vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp of ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Salt
  • Your choice of bread
  • 1 tbsp of unsalted butter (Add more as needed)
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk the milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together.
  3. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet on medium heat
  4. Dunk your choice of bread into mixture, making sure to soak both sides of the bread.
  5. Transfer your bread to hot skillet and cook until golden. (Usually 3 to 4 minutes)
  6. Serve while hot. (with butter and syrup if you so wish)
