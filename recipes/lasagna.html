Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a very yummy, layered dish. Its can take quite a bit of time to prepare but the wait is WORTH it! Lasagna can feed as many or as few people as you need it to, and is very customizable ingredients-wise.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Sweet Italian Sausage
  • 3/4lb Lean Ground Beef
  • 1/2 Minced Onion
  • 2 Garlic cloves, crushed
  • 2 cans of Tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of Tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup of Water
  • 2 tbsp of Sugar
  • 4 tbsp of Fresh parsley
  • 1 1/2 tsp of Dried basil leaves
  • 1 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1 tsp of Italian Seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp of Fennel Seeds
  • 1/4 tso Black Pepper
  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 3/4 cup of Parmesan
  • 3/4 lb of Mozzarella
  • 16 oz of Ricotta cheese
  • 1 Egg (if you wish to add to the ricotta)
Steps to Make Lasagna
  1. Get all ingredients ready and close by
  2. Cook ground beef, sausuage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water
  4. Season with sugar, parsley, basil, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper
  5. Let it simmer while covered for 1 1/2 hours, and stir occasionally
  6. Bring large pot of salted water to boil, add lasagna noodles to water for 10 minutes
  7. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water.
  8. combine ricotta cheese, egg, parsley and salt together in a mixing bowl
  9. preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  10. Spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce onto the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish
  11. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping them slightly
  12. Spread 1/2 of ricotta cheese mix
  13. Top with 1/3 of Mozzarella slices
  14. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over Mozzarella
  15. Sprinkle 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  16. Repeat Steps 11-15
  17. Top with remaining Mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  18. Cover with foil and put into preheated oven for 25 minutes
  19. Remove foil and let bake for another 25 minutes
  20. Let sit for 15 minutes before serving
